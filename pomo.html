POMO


Our app is a time and behavior management application designed for children ages 4-10.
This concept is based on the gamification of the pomodoro method in order to incentivise
children for completing assigned task in a timely fashion. My first objective was to create
and develop a brand identity. Once the brand was established, I developed low and high
fidelity wireframes. The wireframes were then translated into HTML5 and CSS3.

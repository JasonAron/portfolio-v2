GitHubFolio


Our app is a portfolio building app for developers. GitHubFolio automatically generates a
beautiful portfolio with your work. Sign into our app with GitHub and GitHubFolio takes your
six pinned repos and dynamically creates a custom portfolio with your best work. My role in
the development of GitHubFolio is to create the brand identity, develop and implement the UI/UX.
